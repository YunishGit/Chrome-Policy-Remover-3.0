## üõ† About

Chrome Policy Remover is used when Chrome displays messages like **"Your browser is managed by your organization"** or when unwanted pop-ups and restrictions appear. It removes enforced policies, restoring full control over Chrome settings.
Chrome Policy Remover is a powerful and easy-to-use batch script designed to **remove unwanted Chrome policies** that may have been enforced by organizations or malware. This tool helps users regain full control over their Google Chrome settings.

- Removes restrictive Chrome policies
- Deletes registry entries and group policies
- Restores Chrome to default policy settings
- Simple, fast, and effective!

---

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Important Instructions

### This tool must be **run with administrative privileges** while Chrome is in an open/running state.

**Steps to Run:**

1. **Keep Google Chrome running** (Do not close it).
2. **Right-click** on `ChromePolicyRemover.bat`.
3. Select **Run as administrator**.
4. The script will execute and remove all unwanted policies.
5. Once completed, a sound notification will play.
6. Press any key to exit.

---

## üîß How It Works

This script performs the following actions:

- **Closes Chrome processes** (if running in the background)
- **Removes Group Policies** related to Chrome
- **Deletes restrictive registry keys** that enforce unwanted settings
- **Refreshes system policies (gpupdate /force)**
- **Plays a success sound notification** once finished
